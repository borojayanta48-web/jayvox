Jayanta Boro

Jayanta Boro is an independent singer and music enthusiast from Assam, India. Through JayVox, he shares karaoke performances, cover songs, and musical experiments across Hindi, English, Nepali, and Assamese languages.

JayVox represents his passion for music, creativity, and continuous learning. The platform allows listeners to experience songs performed with personal expression while exploring different musical styles and cultures.

Music has always been a way of expression and connection. Jayanta continues to develop his singing journey while building JayVox as a growing digital music space for listeners and future collaborations.
