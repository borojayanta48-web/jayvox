About Jayanta

Jayanta Boro is an independent music enthusiast and passionate singer from Assam,(BTR)Tangla,India. Through JayVox, he shares his dedication to music with listeners who connect with emotional and heartfelt performances.

From Hindi melodies and Nepali classics to English tracks, Jayanta enjoys exploring different musical styles while continuously improving his vocal abilities. For him, singing is more than a hobby — it is a powerful form of self-expression and creativity.

JayVox represents his evolving musical journey. Every recording reflects effort, learning, and the dream of building a recognizable digital music identity. With consistency and passion, he aims to grow as an artist and reach a wider audience through online platforms.

Music is not just sound — it is emotion, dedication, and connection. Jayanta continues to perform, improve, and share his journey with those who support independent artists.
